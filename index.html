About Me

I bridge experimental biology with bioinformatics, machine learning, and predictive modeling. With a Ph.D. in Genetics and Molecular Biology and a specialization in Big Data and Deep Learning, I apply modern AI/ML frameworks to multi-omics datasets, geospatial forecasting, and graph-based learning. My passion lies in transforming complex biological data into actionable insights for precision agriculture, biotechnology, and medical applications.
